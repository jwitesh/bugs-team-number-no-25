Class 7 Civics

The Role of Government

Definition: The government is a system or group of people governing an organized community, often a state.

Functions: Making and enforcing laws, managing resources, and providing public services.

Understanding Democracy

Definition: Democracy is a form of government where the people have the authority to make decisions through elected representatives.

Features: Free and fair elections, separation of powers, and protection of individual rights.

The Indian Constitution

Overview: The Constitution is the supreme law of India that outlines the framework for the government and the fundamental rights and duties of citizens.

Features: Preamble, Fundamental Rights, and Directive Principles of State Policy.

Rights and Responsibilities of Citizens

Rights: Fundamental rights guaranteed by the Constitution such as the right to equality, freedom of speech, and right to education.

Responsibilities: Duties like respecting the law, paying taxes, and participating in community activities.

The Local Government System

Structure: Local governments include municipalities and panchayats.

Functions: Local governance involves managing local resources, implementing policies, and addressing community issues.

The Importance of Civic Participation

Definition: Civic participation involves engaging in activities that contribute to the betterment of society, such as voting, volunteering, and community service.

Impact: Enhances democratic processes and fosters a sense of community.
