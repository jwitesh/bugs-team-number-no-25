Class 7 Economics

Understanding Economic Activities

Definition: Economic activities involve the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services.

Types: Primary (agriculture, mining), secondary (manufacturing), and tertiary (services) sectors.

Types of Resources

Natural Resources: Resources that occur naturally such as land, water, minerals, and forests.

Human Resources: The labor force and skills of people.

Capital Resources: Machinery, buildings, and technology used in production.

Production and Factors of Production

Production: The process of creating goods and services.

Factors of Production: Land, labor, capital, and entrepreneurship.

Consumption and Its Importance

Definition: Consumption is the use of goods and services by people.

Importance: Drives economic growth and influences demand for products.

Economic Systems

Types: Market economy, command economy, and mixed economy.

Features: How different systems manage resources and economic activities.

Economic Development

Definition: The process of improving the economic well-being and quality of life for a community or country.

Indicators: GDP, literacy rates, and access to healthcare.

Role of Government in the Economy

Functions: Regulation, taxation, and public expenditure.

Impact: Influences economic stability and growth.
