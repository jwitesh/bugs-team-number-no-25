Class 6 Social Science

The Earth in the Solar System

This topic covers the position of the Earth in the solar system, the concept of planets, and how the Earth orbits the Sun. It includes understanding the relationship between the Earth, Moon, and other celestial bodies.

Globe: Latitude and Longitude

Learn about the Globe, the concept of latitude and longitude, and how these coordinates are used to locate places on the Earth. This section introduces the basics of cartography and global positioning.

Maps and Their Uses

This section explores different types of maps, their uses, and how they help us understand geographic information. It includes an introduction to physical, political, and thematic maps.

Major Landforms of the Earth

Understand the various landforms found on Earth such as mountains, plains, plateaus, and valleys. This topic explains their formation and significance in the Earth's surface structure.

Our Environment

Explore the components of the environment including the atmosphere, hydrosphere, lithosphere, and biosphere. This section emphasizes the importance of environmental conservation.

Physical Features of India

This topic discusses the diverse physical features of India such as the Himalayas, the Ganges Plain, the Thar Desert, and the Coastal Plains. It highlights the impact of these features on climate and human activities.

Climate and Vegetation

Learn about the various climates of India and the corresponding vegetation types. This section covers the impact of climate on agriculture and lifestyle.

The Indian Economy

This topic provides an overview of the Indian economy, including its primary sectors like agriculture, industry, and services. It explains economic terms and concepts relevant to India.

Understanding Diversity

Explore the concept of diversity in India, including cultural, religious, and linguistic diversity. This section emphasizes the significance of understanding and respecting different cultures.

The Role of Government

This topic explains the role of government in society, including the functions of different branches of government and how they work to maintain law and order and provide public services.
